Gala

A Free Bird is committed to empowering children battling cancer to fly free through the arts. We have chosen the theme Diamonds for this years gala to symbolize our mission of helping children with cancer achieve their artistic potential of shining as bright and being as strong as a diamond. Through your support of our gala, you help us accomplish this mission.

The Cullinan

The single largest diamond ever found, The Cullinan was discovered in South Africa in 1905. Also called The Star of Africa, the stone is peerless in beauty as well as size. It was gifted to King Edward VII and is currently mounted in the Sovereign’s Scepter with Cross. Your gift of $5,000 gives you access to the highest tier of sponsorship and includes a reserved table for 10, your company logo in the gala program, prominent placement of your name at the event, and acknowledgement of your organization from the podium.

Purchase The Cullinan Ticket

Lesedi La Rona

The second largest diamond ever found, Lesedi La Rona was discovered in Botswana in 2015. Its name, which means "Our Light" in the Tswana language, was given to it because "the diamond is a pride, light and hope of Botswana. Your gift of $3,500 gives you access to this tier of sponsorship and includes a reserved table for 5, your company logo in the program, and prominent placement of your name at the event.

Purchase Lesedi La Rona Ticket

The Excelsior

The third largest diamond ever found, The Excelsior was discovered in South Africa in 1893. Its name, which means "higher," refers to its spectacular quality. Your gift of $2,500 gives you access to this tier of sponsorship and includes 3 tickets to the gala and a listing of you and your company logo in the gala program.

Purchase The Excelsior Ticket

The Star of Sierra Leone

The fourth largest diamond ever found, The Star of Sierra Leone is a breathtaking gem discovered in Sierra Leone in 1972. It has been ranked in the top 1% of all diamonds in terms of its chemical purity. Your gift of $1,000 gives you access to this tier of sponsorship and 2 tickets to the gala.
